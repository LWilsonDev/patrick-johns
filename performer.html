Performer

Patrick has been a professional trombonist for 20 years, playing in a wide variety of ensemble, either as a regular member or as a guest, including:

  • Oompah Brass, the UK’s leading Oktoberfest band and exponents of Oompop
  • Big-name acts such as Michael Ball, Robert Lindsay and Emma Thompson
  • Bands such as Chas ’n’ Dave and Hayseed Dixie
  • The Noblemen and Gentlemen’s Catch Club
  • Orchestras, including the Symphony Orchestra of India
  • Ska acts, including Prince Buster and The Dualers (including two top 25 hits)
  • London Vegetable Orchestra, playing 1st Butternut Squash

Patrick has performed at all the main London jazz and classical venues, and at many UK festivals such at Bestival, Isle of Wight Festival and the London Jazz Festival.

TV work includes Odd One In (ITV), Mad Bad Ad Show (Channel 4), whilst advertising work includes H&M (with Tony Bennett and Lady Gaga), Johnnie Walker (with Jude Law), and Victim Support (national campaign).

Patrick starred in two 5* sell-out shows at the Edinburgh Fringe: “The A to Z of Oompah”, and “Blow Up! The Credit Crunch Musical”, which won praise across the regional and national press.

He also gigs (less frequently!) on bass guitar, drums, tuba and guitar (his singing days are mostly behind him).
