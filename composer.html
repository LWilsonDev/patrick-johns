Arranger - Composer

Patrick is very active as a music arranger, in many different styles.

He has arranged hundreds of songs and pieces for brass quintet, and for large brass ensemble (published by Superbrass Publishing), and for choir and young voices (published by Sing Up).

In 2017, Patrick had the pleasure of arranging 20 Elvis Presley songs for full symphony orchestra, for concerts given by the world’s no.1 Elvis tribute, Gordon Hendricks, entitled “Symphonic Elvis”.

And in 2014, he arranged and performed an orchestral version of Bohemian Rhapsody with Rainer Hersch, the London Firebird Orchestra, and the Crouch End Festival Chorus, at the Royal Festival Hall.

TV arranging work includes Last Choir Standing (BBC 1) and the Mad Bad Ad Show (Channel 4).

Patrick creates bespoke wedding arrangements for the company My Way Music
