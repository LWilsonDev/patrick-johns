Patrick Johns

Producer - Presenter - Musician - Educator

About Patrick

Patrick is a freelance musician with a varied portfolio ranging from producing hit radio shows for the BBC, advisor to EDEXCEL A-level music, performing across the globe, and writing a sell-out comedy show for the Edinburgh Fringe.

After studing music at Nottingham University, Patrick went on to work for Grove Music Dictionary before becoming a producer at the BBC. Patrick was also forging a freelance career as a profesional trombonist, performing and recording for popular Ska band, "Brass Volcanoes", reaching no.1 in the charts.
