Presenter and Producer

Patrick has worked as a freelance Radio Presenter and Producer since 2007, before which he worked in production at BBC Radio 3 across programmes such as Jazz Record Requests, In Tune, Voices, Hear & Now, and Late Junction.

Podcast

Patrick currently presents and produces the UK’s no.1 Music Education podcast, Teaching Notes, having interviewed over 100 musicians and educators, including Julian Lloyd Webber, John Rutter, Katie Melua, Guy Chambers, Nicola Benedetti, Ruthie Henshall, Bob Chilcott, Dick Hyman, Ron Carter, and Steve Hackett.
Available on Apple, Spotify, Buzzsprout, and more.
